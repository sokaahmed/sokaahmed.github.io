Section 01

The App does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number to use its core features.

However, the App and its integrated third-party services may automatically collect certain non-personally identifying information as described below:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, language setting, and screen resolution.
  • App Usage Data: Feature interactions, session duration, and app navigation patterns used to improve user experience.
  • Advertising Identifiers: Google Advertising ID (GAID) and other resettable ad identifiers used for ad delivery and measurement.
  • Crash & Diagnostic Data: App crashes, error logs, and performance metrics to help us diagnose and fix issues.
  • Network Information: IP address and general connection type (Wi-Fi / mobile) used for service delivery and security.
  • Downloaded Content Metadata: File names and formats of media you choose to download, stored locally on your device only.
We do not collect sensitive personal information such as precise location, contacts, messages, browsing history, health data, or financial information.

Section 02

Media & File Access (Device Permissions)

To save and display the videos and images you download, the App may request the following device permissions. These are the only permissions we request.

🖼️
Photos / Media Storage
Required to save downloaded videos and images to your device's gallery. On Android 13+, this maps to READ_MEDIA_VIDEO and READ_MEDIA_IMAGES. On older versions, it maps to READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE.
🌐
Internet Access
Required to fetch video content and serve advertisements. This is a standard permission and does not expose personal data.
✅  We do not access: Contacts, SMS/MMS, microphone, camera, location, call logs, calendar, or any unrelated personal files on your device. Downloaded media is stored locally on your device only and is never uploaded to our servers.
